Meet The Sapphire Design Group

We are the executives of Sapphire Design Group, a team of architects who love creating buildings and improving lives. We set out with the objective of accomplishing projects that bring a smile to our customer's faces. And with our decades of experience, we guarantee quality.

Broderick Cumberbatch

Brodcerick studied architecture during his undergraduate at Hogwart's School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He later returned for a MBA from Hogwart's School of Business. Inspired by the people he met there, Broderick co-founded Sapphire Design Group to pursue his dream of leading an innovative architecture company.

Wilbert Summerbee

Wilbert was one of Broderick's classmates at Hogwart's School of Business. Sharing Broderick's vision of excellence, Wilbert invested his life savings to co-found Sapphire. Although Wilbert originally studied Defense Against the Dark Arts, he quicky learned the principles of architectures on the job and has been a major factor in the success of the company.

Marlene Pilliwickle

Marlene had worked at the Ministry of Magic for over six years before joining Sapphire as the Chief Technology Officer. She describes the switch as "entering a whole new world" and loves getting to work with a group of intelligent and motivated people.

Tuppence Middleton

Tuppence studied architecture at the Durmstrang Institute and was a starter on the varsity Quidditch team. Tuppence joined Sapphire right after graduation and has happily been here since, specializing in foreign architecture.

Daisy Dodderidge

While initially majoring in potions at Beauxbatons Academy of Magic, Daisy realized that her true passion in life was coming up with styllish designs for buildings. She spent two years studying architecture on her own and it is now one of the main designers at Sapphire.

Kirley McCormack

Kirley is a veteran in the architecture world, having worked for over two decades in the industry. As the Chief Innovation Officer, Kirley brings to us a vast amount of knowledge about both buildings and businesses.
